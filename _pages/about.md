I am a researcher and Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati. My research is at the forefront of next-generation wireless networks, specializing in the application of advanced AI/ML—including Reinforcement Learning, Deep Learning, and Transformer models—to solve complex challenges in 5G/6G systems. My work focuses on pioneering algorithms for interference management, intelligent channel estimation, and resource optimization in NOMA networks to enhance spectral efficiency, mobility resilience, and green communication.

My research contributions, including the development of the MOGA-RL hybrid algorithm and a novel Deep-SIC channel estimator, have been published and are under review in leading international journals and conferences.

## Research Interests

*   **AI/ML for Wireless Communications:** Deep Learning, Reinforcement Learning, and Transformer models for the physical layer.
*   **5G/6G Systems:** Non-Orthogonal Multiple Access (NOMA), massive MIMO, and D2D communication.
*   **Signal Processing & Estimation:** Advanced channel estimation (CSI) and prediction using AI-driven techniques.
*   **Network Optimization:** Dynamic resource allocation, interference mitigation, and QoS enhancement for ultra-dense networks.
*   **Green Communication:** Energy-efficient system design and optimization.
